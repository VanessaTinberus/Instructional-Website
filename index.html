How to make tea eggs?

(An Instructional Recipe)

Tea egg is a famous Chinese street food which can often be found in a big pot at a breakfast shop. They are braised with spices and most of them have a beautiful marbled surface due to the cracked eggshell.

This recipe will show you how to make savory tea eggs step by step, from selecting eggs to soaking eggs in the marinating liquid. Of course, you can change the recipe based on your own preference while cooking, and we'll offer some tips and options in the next few pages.

Here are the steps we'll go through:

  1. Prepare the ingredients
    • Select fresh eggs and the key ingredients used for boiling.
  2. Boil the eggs
    • Boil the eggs in the marinating liquid for a certain period of time.
  3. Soak the eggs
    • Add flavors to the boiled eggs by soaking them in the marinating liquid.
